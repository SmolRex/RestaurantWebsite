Our À La Carte

Welcome to our À La Carte menu, where classic British elegance meets modern culinary artistry. Each dish is crafted with the finest locally sourced ingredients, showcasing the vibrant flavors and rich heritage of British cuisine. From indulgent starters to exquisite main courses and decadent desserts, our offerings celebrate the season's best, with each plate designed to captivate the senses. Whether you're here for a special celebration or simply to savor exceptional flavors, our team is dedicated to delivering an unforgettable dining experience, one that reflects both tradition and innovation. Dive into a world of taste, sophistication, and delight—welcome to the essence of fine British dining.

"Each dish combines the finest locally-sourced ingredients with refined techniques, offering a modern take on beloved British classics. From the sea’s bounty to seasonal game, we invite you to explore the richness and tradition of British cuisine in every bite. Enjoy this journey through taste, thoughtfully created for your dining pleasure."

Master Chef Tan Phat Dang

Executive Chef and Culinary Director
