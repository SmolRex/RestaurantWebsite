Our Story

Royce & Co. embodies a seamless blend of elegance, precision, and legacy. Founded with the vision of creating a unique dining experience, the restaurant embraces the highest standards of craftsmanship in both its culinary offerings and ambiance. Each dish is an artful composition, prepared with meticulous attention to detail, aiming to evoke a sense of refined sophistication. Our team of culinary experts is dedicated to bringing a bold, luxurious approach to every plate, echoing a commitment to quality and innovation. At Royce & Co., guests are invited to experience a world where culinary mastery and timeless style converge, crafting unforgettable moments with each visit.

"In our kitchen, every dish tells a story. We blend traditional techniques with modern innovation to create experiences that delight all the senses."

Master Chef Tan Phat Dang
