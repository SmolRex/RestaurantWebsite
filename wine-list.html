Our Wines

Each bottle has been handpicked for its exceptional quality and ability to enhance the flavors of our cuisine, from timeless classics of France and Germany to the emerging elegance of Vietnam’s finest vineyards. Whether you're savoring a bold red, a refreshing white, or a wine that tells the story of its origin, our collection invites you to indulge in a journey of taste and discovery.

"Our collaboration with the sommelier ensures that the wines we serve not only complement our dishes but elevate them to an unforgettable experience. From delicate seafood to robust cuts of meat, every plate finds its perfect match within our carefully curated wine list."

Master Chef Tan Phat Dang

Executive Chef and Culinary Director
